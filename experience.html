Med-Arb & ADR Expertise

Med-Arb as a Combined Process

The Med-Arb process, where mediation is used before or during arbitration, is becoming increasingly popular as a method of resolving disputes, particularly in commercial settings.

Benefits of Med-Arb

This process aims to encourage parties to settle their differences through mediation before resorting to the more formal and costly process of arbitration, saving time and resources.

Applicability of Med-Arb

The Med-Arb process can be applied to various types of disputes, including investment disputes, commercial contracts, and cross-border matters.
