Med-Arb Process

What is Med-Arb? Hybrid dispute resolution where parties first attempt mediation; if impasse, the same neutral becomes arbitrator and issues binding decision.

1
Initial Consultation
Parties discuss dispute, determine best course of action, sign agreement.
2
Mediation Phase
Neutral facilitator helps parties negotiate, explore settlement options.
3
Settlement Attempt
If parties reach agreement → case closed. If not → move to arbitration.
4
Arbitration Decision
Arbitrator evaluates evidence, hears arguments, issues binding award.
5
Post-Hearing
Written decision provided, support for implementation.

Benefits: Faster resolution, lower cost, confidential process, enforceable outcome.
